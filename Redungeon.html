Re:Duneon

Re:Dungeon was the honours project for my computer games development degree, it's a turn-based isometric shooter inspired by some of my personal favourite turn-based games; Fallout 1, Baldur's Gate 3 and Steampunk Heist.

The game was made to test methods of improving player engagement and experience using differently implemented rewards or the lack of rewards in ways that could motivate or demotivate players to engage in different areas of gameplay or the map, this was paired with a research paper looking over the psychological research on rewards and human behaviour.

The paper and game were graded together a 97% (im sorry, i have to mention it)
